Welcome to the Artistry of Jingdezhen Porcelain

Welcome to Timeless Treasures, where the exquisite craftsmanship of Jingdezhen porcelain comes to life. This virtual museum showcases three iconic artifacts from China's Qing Dynasty, offering an immersive experience through detailed 3D models.

Featured Artifacts

Artifact 1: Qing Dynasty Covered Baluster Vase

This covered baluster vase is part of a five-piece garniture from the Qing dynasty, dating from the Kangxi reign (1662-1722). It is crafted from white porcelain with cobalt pigment under a clear glaze.
